Your Perfect Brew Awaits.

Welcome to Coffee & Brew, where every cup is crafted from ethically sourced beans roasted in‑house for maximum flavor. Enjoy a velvety latte, bright pour‑over, or bold espresso alongside fresh pastries made daily by local artisans. Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or simply unwind, our cozy space and free Wi‑Fi await.

Menu

Our menu starts each day with flaky, butter-rich croissants, artisan muffins, and savory breakfast sandwiches. Midday brings handcrafted paninis and grain bowls piled with seasonal veggies, locally sourced cheeses, and your choice of roasted meats or plant-based proteins. A rotating selection of sweet treats—think indulgent brownies, fruit-topped tarts, and vegan cookies—makes the perfect companion for any cup.
